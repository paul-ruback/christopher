Specifications

Physical Design

  • • Size: 7.5" x 5" x 0.6"
  • • Weight: ~350g
  • • Screen: 6.8" e-ink, glare-free
  • • Keyboard: Mechanical scissor-switch
  • • Body: Matte magnesium alloy
  • • Colors: Graphite, Ivory, Moss

Technical Specs

  • • OS: Minimalist Linux firmware
  • • Processor: ARM Cortex A53
  • • RAM: 1GB LPDDR3
  • • Storage: 8GB internal eMMC
  • • Battery: 4000mAh, 3-4 weeks
  • • Ports: USB-C, SD card slot

Writing Experience

  • • Formats: Plaintext, Markdown, RTF
  • • Tools: Word count, reading time
  • • Split screen for notes/outlines
  • • Auto-save every 10 seconds
  • • Night mode with adjustable warmth
  • • Focus timers for writing sessions

Philosophy & Approach

  • • No WiFi or cloud connectivity
  • • Zero notifications or distractions
  • • Text-focused writing environment
  • • Local-first file management
  • • Privacy by design architecture
  • • Sustainable long-term use focus
